Registration Information

Thank you for your interest in participating in ETMUN 2025! Registration is open to high school students (ages 14-18) from all schools. Please read the following information carefully before completing the registration form.

Important Dates

  • Early Registration Deadline: June 30, 2025
  • Regular Registration Deadline: August 31, 2025
  • Conference Dates: October 15-17, 2025

Registration Fees

  • Early Registration: £50 per delegate
  • Regular Registration: £65 per delegate
  • School Delegation (10+ delegates): 10% discount

Fee includes conference materials, lunch, and refreshments during the conference.

What to Prepare

  • Position paper on your committee topics
  • Research on your assigned country
  • Formal attire for the conference
  • Notebook and writing materials

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I submit my registration? +

After submitting your registration, you will receive a confirmation email within 48 hours. This email will include payment instructions and next steps. Your registration is not complete until payment is received.

Can I request a specific country or committee? +

Yes, you can indicate your preferences in the registration form. While we try to accommodate preferences, assignments are made on a first-come, first-served basis, so early registration is recommended.

Is there a refund policy? +

Refunds are available for cancellations made before September 15, 2025, with a 25% administrative fee. No refunds will be issued after this date, but substitutions are allowed.

Do you provide accommodation for delegates? +

ETMUN does not provide accommodation, but we can recommend nearby hotels and hostels that offer special rates for conference participants. Please contact us for more information.

About ETMUN

English Talents Model United Nations is an educational conference that simulates the United Nations to develop students' diplomatic and leadership skills.
