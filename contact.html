Frequently Asked Questions

When will I receive a response to my inquiry? +

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our phone number directly.

How can I join the ETMUN organizing team? +

We welcome enthusiastic individuals to join our organizing team! Please send an email to etmun@gmail.com with the subject "Joining ETMUN Team" and include your resume and a brief statement explaining why you'd like to join and what skills you can contribute.

Can I visit the ETMUN office in person? +

Yes, you can visit our office during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM). We recommend scheduling an appointment in advance to ensure that someone is available to assist you.

How can my school participate in ETMUN? +

Schools interested in participating in ETMUN should contact us via email at etmun@gmail.com. We'll provide information about registration, fees, and the number of delegates your school can send. We encourage early registration as spots fill up quickly.

About ETMUN

English Talents Model United Nations is an educational conference that simulates the United Nations to develop students' diplomatic and leadership skills.
